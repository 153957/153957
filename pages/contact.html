About Me

I am Arne de Laat, a photography enthusiast living in the Netherlands. My photography started in 2004 with the purchase of a compact camera, some years later upgrading to DSLR cameras. Soon after I started with photography I also discovered the wonderful world of time-lapse. In an effort to share my experience I added a the time-lapse guide to this site for other people interested in time-lapse.

My interests in photography subjects are vast, but as can be seen from my photos here my main subjects are nature (flora and fauna), events (festivals and balfolk dance).
