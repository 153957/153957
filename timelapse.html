This is my Time-lapse shwowcase. This page contains a collection of my Time-lapse movies. Most movies were created with my Nikon D80 DSLR and some with either my Nikon D700 or Canon Powershot S60. For the Nikon D80 I used an external intervalometer or a computer to control the camera, the Canon S60 and Nikon D700 have a built-in intervalometer to take timelapse photos. An HTML5 video capable browser or Quicktime 7+ is required to view the movies, the movies are encoded with the x264/WebM/Ogv codecs (the most suitable is selected by your browser). The movies are ordered by shooting date, the newest are at the top of the categories. Most movies are made in the Netherlands, but some were shot in Chile, Japan or China. The categories include movies about archery, balfolk dancing, blooming flowers, airplanes, traffic, fireshows, clouds, sunsets, sunrise, night skies, stars, ice cubes melting and some other miscellaneous subjects.
